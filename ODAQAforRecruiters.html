A: Reference this document to understand the phases and states after Offer acceptance: 06 2nd Pass, Moving to HR, and Filling Requisitions.pptx

 

4.       Q: How do I remove/delete a pending worker who will not be starting with us?

A: Please submit a Service Now ticket with the name and person number of the pending worker requesting them to be purged

 

5.       Q: My candidate cannot login to complete their preboarding journey. / My candidate did not receive the journey email

A: First please try to resend the journey email with their username and password to them by following these instructions: Resend Journey Email.docx. If they still cannot login please submit a service now ticket with their name and person number requesting for a password to be created for them.

 

6.       Q: Cannot reject/ disposition my candidate in 2nd pass

A: please submit a service now ticket requesting our team to disposition the candidate and include the following information: candidate name, candidate number, requisition number, and the disposition reason

 

7.       Q: How do I bypass an approver? How can I reassign an approval?

A: Please submit a service now ticket requesting our team to bypass or reassign the approval with the requestion number or the person name on the offer.
